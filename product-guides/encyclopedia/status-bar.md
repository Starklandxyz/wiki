# Status Bar

The status bar at the top displays the following in order:&#x20;

* **Player name**
* **Food** &#x20;
* **Gold**
* **Iron**
* **Occupied plots/Current level's maximum**&#x20;
* **Occupied plots current soldiers/Current level's maximum soldiers allowed**
* **Dominion power**
* **Number of Lucky Packs**
* **Ranking**
